Welcome to Nkwazi Community School

Nkwazi Community School, is located in the heart of Ndola, Zambia, offers a high-quality education to pupils from all walks of life.

Why Donate to us?

Our community school is a vital resource for our pupils, families, and community. We provide a high-quality education to all pupils, regardless of their background or abilities. We also offer a wide range of programs and resources to support our students and families.We are committed to using all donations wisely and responsibly

Our school is a caring and secure environment where all children can develop their full potential.
We are committed to facilitating a happy and stimulating academic and social environment for all Pupils.
