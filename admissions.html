How to Apply

① Inquire

Make an inquiry on the availability of school places

② Apply

Download the application form, quickly fill-in and submit via email, or apply directly online

③ Interview

Bring the prospective student to be interviewed. If the child passes the interview for the grade applied for, proceed to Step 4!

④ Pay School Fees

Make payment at the bank and return the bank deposit slip. A receipt for payment is then issued. Voil√†! Simple as 1, 2, 3, 4!

Note! Those who apply when places are full are put on waiting list and are served first as they come and as places become available.
